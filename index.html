How to Play Steal a Brainrot

Master the game with these essential tips and controls

Steal Brainrot Online is a chaotic multiplayer heist game where you collect and protect rare Brainrot memes like Tralalero Tralala and Cappuccino Assassino. Buy memes from the feed, sneak into rival bases to steal theirs, and defend your own with traps and locks. Use boosters, outbid opponents mid-delivery, and rebirth for bigger bonuses as you climb to meme-collecting supremacy.

This is an alternative version of the game by the name "Steal a Brainrot" which is on Roblox.

PC Controls

  • WASD = Move character
  • Space = Jump
  • E = Interact with objects
  • Mouse = Look around

Mobile Controls

  • Left Joystick = Move character
  • Swipe = Look around
  • Up Arrow = Jump
  • Hand Icon = Interact

Settings & Tips

  • Adjust mouse sensitivity in settings
  • Modify graphics quality for performance
  • Control music and sound volume
  • Use traps and locks to protect your memes
  • Rebirth for bigger bonuses and advantages
